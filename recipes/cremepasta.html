Recipe for Creme Pasta

Creme pasta is easy to make but delicious

If you are craving a pasta dish, try this out!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Creme cheese
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook pasta for 8 minutes
  2. Put 100ml of milk and 3 quarters of creme cheese. Add a bit of cheese
  3. Mix cooked pasta and 50mL of pasta water into the milk and creme cheese
  4. Mix well
  5. Enjoy
