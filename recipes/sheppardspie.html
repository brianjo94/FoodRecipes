Sheppard's Pie

Choice of minced beef or lamb mixed with green beans, carrots served with overlay of mash potatoes

Shepherd's pie, cottage pie, or in its French version hachis Parmentier is a savoury dish of cooked minced meat topped with mashed potato and baked.

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Carrots
  • Tomato paste
  • Potato
  • Green beans

Steps

  1. Mince up some beef
  2. Chop up some veggies
  3. Put them all together
  4. Put potatoes ontop of everything
  5. Enjoy
